# Support & Feedback

Thank you for using **Store Pal**. We are committed to providing a simple, privacy-focused shopping experience.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Where is my data stored?
Your shopping lists are stored directly on your device and in your personal iCloud account. We do not have servers, and we never have access to your data.

### How does list sharing work?
Sharing is powered by Apple's iCloud. When you invite someone to a list, they receive a secure link. You can view who has access or revoke access at any time by tapping the "Share" icon within any list.

### I’m having trouble syncing across devices.
Since the app uses iCloud, please ensure:
1. You are signed into the same Apple ID on all devices.
2. iCloud Drive is enabled in your device Settings.
3. Store Pal is toggled "On" in your iCloud settings.
